Daily Nutrition Basics

Keep meals simple: include protein, fiber-rich carbs, healthy fats, and veggies.

Macro checklist

  • Protein: ~20–40g per meal
  • Carbs: whole grains, fruit, potatoes
  • Fats: olive oil, nuts, avocado

Labels you’ll see

RPE
Rating of Perceived Exertion (effort 1–10).
Maintenance
Calories to keep your current weight.

Food group guidance: USDA MyPlate food groups.
