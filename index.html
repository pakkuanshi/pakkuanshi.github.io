Training Record Website

Welcome! This site helps you log workouts and meals, set goals, and learn what to eat to reach them.

What you can do here

  • Track your daily workouts and meals.
  • Set clear goals and monitor progress.
  • Learn simple, sustainable nutrition principles.

Key terms

BMI
Body Mass Index, a weight-to-height ratio used as a general indicator.
Macros
Macronutrients: protein, carbohydrates, and fats.
Progressive overload
Gradually increasing training stimulus to improve performance.
